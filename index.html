When Two Souls Met in the Gym — Our Love Story


They say love finds you when you least expect it. For me, it happened on 28th June 2025, inside a place meant for breaking limits — the gym. I didn’t know that day I’d end up meeting someone who’d redefine what “strength” really means — not just physical, but emotional, too. That was the day I met Himanshi.

It wasn’t long before I realized I wasn’t the only one feeling that magnetic pull. Slowly, we began noticing each other more — those small smiles, shared playlists, casual gym talk that somehow turned into late-night conversations. And then it happened — we both fell in love.

What started as two people crossing paths at the gym turned into a story neither of us could’ve written better. Because sometimes, love doesn’t need grand gestures or perfect timing. Sometimes, it just takes one look, one shared moment, and the courage to let it grow.

Here’s to us — to all the happy moments we’ve shared, and to all the beautiful memories we’re yet to create together. ❤️
